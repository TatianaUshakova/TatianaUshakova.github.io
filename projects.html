Projects

A collection of tools and research projects I'm working on.

Robotics Projects

Working with the SO-arm 101 robotics arm on various projects, including a minimal version of a robotics cooker, and exploring whether the Karpathy-style autoresearch loop can extend to robotics - a nontrivial task because it needs tight iteration loops and can't be easily parallelized, but there's some hope for meaningful output from partial automation.

Theoretical-physics-translate-LLM

Coming from a theoretical physics background, many radar-related books were annoying to read because they contained too much text description and weren't dense enough on formulas, meaning I spent much more time reading than I would on a few dense pages of PDF. I'm building a tool that converts any-topic PDFs into a style natural to a theoretical physicist.

The natural extension would be a tool that takes any arXiv paper and rewrites it to another arXiv paper in the domain language of the reader, eliminating cognitive barriers to reading cross-domain publications.

Time-spent-advisor

Most productivity tools tell you the breakdown of time spent on apps but give very little actionable information on what to do with it. I spent a lot of time analyzing my time use manually, asking "what exactly did I do ('submitted a refund for a trip', not 'answered emails'), what was the purpose of this action, and is it the best way to spend my time?", which gave me a lot of useful information for improving my productivity.

Now I'm building an app that does the same automatically.
